How to use the app

Tag your matches — live from the sideline on a phone, or afterwards from video — and the analysis builds itself: dashboards, shot maps, kickout maps, win benchmarks, scorers and more. This guide covers everything, but you only need the bits that apply to you.

01 · EVERYONE

Getting started

  1. Open the app: rocksolidanalytics.github.io/rocksolidanalytics-app
  2. On your phone, tap the menu (⋮) and Add to Home screen for an app icon and full-screen view. Optional — if your phone won’t allow it, just bookmark the link, it works exactly the same.
  3. Sign up with your email address.
  4. Choose your path: players tap the green “Join my club” card; analysts set up (or continue with) the club below it.

There are two kinds of account. Analysts tag matches and manage the club. Players can view the dashboards and tactical pad. You’ll pick which when you join.

02 · PLAYERS

Joining as a player

  1. Sign up, then on the first screen tap “I’m a player — join my club”.
  2. Enter the club code your analyst gives you (it’s on their home screen).
  3. That’s it — you land straight into the dashboards.

As a player you’ll see the Dashboard (all the team stats and season story), the Tactical pad, and Help. Have a browse — the “What Wins Look Like” section is a good place to start.

03 · ANALYSTS

Squad & team selection

Add your squad
Home → Squad. Add every player by name — takes a couple of minutes. New signings can be added any time.
Preferred positions
Tap a player’s position chip to set where they usually play. This powers smarter suggestions when you pick a team.
Building a teamsheet
Before a match, the picker suggests the right players for each position — based on where they’ve actually played and their preferred position.
04 · ANALYSTS

Tagging live from the sideline

The live tagger is built for one-handed use at speed. Create the match (Home → New match — set competition, home/away, weather), then tag as the game unfolds.

The two-thumb flow
Tap an event (Kickout, Shot…), then its outcome, then the player number. Tap = your team, double-tap = opposition.
Playing direction
A banner shows which way you’re attacking, and flips automatically at half time. Tap SWAP if needed.
Cards & sin-bins
Tag a card and it marks the player — yellow, greyed-out for a red, or a live 10-minute countdown for a black.
Substitutions
The ⇄ SUB button swaps players in and out; pick the incoming player by name or number.
Fix a mistake
Tap any tag in the list to edit its outcome or player, or delete it. Nothing is ever stuck.
Never lose a tag
Once an event has an outcome it saves automatically — even if you don’t get time to add a player.
05 · ANALYSTS

Tagging from video

Same tagging, done afterwards from a match video (your own Dropbox or a local file). Rotate to landscape for the full-screen tagging view.

Full-screen video
In landscape the video fills the screen with a small scoreboard in the corner. Tap ⛶ to hide the browser bar completely.
The tag strip
The bar along the bottom is the tagger — events, then outcomes, then players, all inline. It tucks away between tags.
Jumping around
≪10s / 10s≫ buttons skip through dead time; −60s…+60s and the ± fine controls live in the ≡ menu, along with the throw-in and 2nd-half markers.
Mark the halves
Set the throw-in and 2nd-half start once (in the ≡ menu) so the match clock lines up with the video.
06 · ANALYSTS

Live scoreboard for supporters

Tap Share link beside the Live mirror card on your home screen, and send it to your WhatsApp group. Anyone with the link sees a live scoreboard — score, scorers and recent events — updating every few seconds as you tag. No login needed, so it’s perfect for supporters and family who can’t make the game.

07 · EVERYONE

Reading the dashboards

Everything below updates automatically from your tagged matches.

Competition filter
The chips at the top — ALL / LEAGUE / CHAMPIONSHIP / CHALLENGE — split every stat below them, so you can compare league form to championship form in one tap.
Season Read & Snapshot
A plain-English summary of the season, plus record, averages and score share.
What Wins Look Like
The numbers that separate your wins from your defeats — with a clear target for each. The heart of the app.
Comparison & maps
Your team vs opposition across every metric, plus shot maps and kickout maps on a real pitch.
Source of Scores
Where each of your scores began — own kickout, opposition kickout, or turnover.
Conditions & scorers
How you perform home/away and in different weather, and your top scorers in G-P notation.
08 · ANALYSTS

Opposition scouting

Video Hub → Opposition Scouting lets you tag a match between two other teams — fully custom names and numbers — to build a report on an upcoming opponent. It gets its own analysis dashboard (the gold STATS button) and is kept completely separate from your own team’s stats.

09 · EVERYONE

Help & handy tips

Ask the assistant
Home → Help & support. Ask anything about the app and get an instant answer; anything it can’t answer goes to your analyst.
Seeing a new feature?
If an update doesn’t show, pull down to refresh. On the installed app, close it fully and reopen.
Video won’t load?
For Dropbox links, add ?dl=1 to the end of the share link, or pick a local file.
That’s the whole app. Tag the game; the analysis builds itself. Questions? Use Help & support inside the app, or ask your analyst.
